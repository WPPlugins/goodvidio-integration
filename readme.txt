**Features**

Goodvidio gives you an easy-to-use app that combines everything you need to successfully use product videos from social media to improve conversion on your WooCommerce store.

* Automatic discovery of product pages on your WooCommerce store that can be enhanced with videos.
* Automatic  search and filtering of product videos for your SKUs, based on your preferences (i.e. type of video creator, language).
* Goodvidio Score tells you the quality and relevance of each discovered product video. No guesswork.
* Automatic publishing of the best videos to your product pages. No manual embedding or coding required.
* Beautiful and engaging video galleries added to your product pages and automatically optimized for mobile devices.
* Videos open up on the page in an overlay. Integration with YouTube.
* Automatic replacement of videos that are no longer available with new content. No broken video links on product pages.
* Shopper engagement & conversion insights delivered straight to your email by Goodvidio Insights. Stay on top on how videos perform on your store.
* A/B testing that helps you optimize for conversions and engagement.
